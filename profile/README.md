# Penn Neuroscience Graduate Center (PNGC)

## About Us

The Penn Neuroscience Graduate Center (PNGC) at the University of Pennsylvania is a premier research and educational institution dedicated to advancing the understanding of the nervous system through innovative research, interdisciplinary collaboration, and world-class training. We bring together faculty, students, and researchers from diverse fields to tackle fundamental questions in neuroscience and translate discoveries into clinical applications.

Our mission is to foster a collaborative environment that promotes excellence in neuroscience research, education, and training while preparing the next generation of neuroscience leaders.

## Vision & Mission

**Vision:** To be a leading center for neuroscience research and education, driving discoveries that transform our understanding of the brain and nervous system.

**Mission:** 
- Conduct cutting-edge research across all areas of neuroscience
- Provide exceptional graduate training and mentorship
- Foster interdisciplinary collaboration and innovation
- Translate scientific discoveries into clinical and societal impact
- Build a diverse and inclusive community of neuroscience scholars

## Research Areas

Our research spans multiple domains of neuroscience, including:
- Systems and Behavioral Neuroscience
- Cellular and Molecular Neuroscience
- Computational and Theoretical Neuroscience
- Cognitive Neuroscience
- Clinical and Translational Neuroscience
- Neural Engineering and Technology

## Training

The PNGC is committed to providing comprehensive training opportunities for neuroscience researchers at all career stages.

### Graduate Programs
- **Ph.D. Program in Neuroscience:** Comprehensive doctoral training with rotations across multiple labs
- **Combined Degree Programs:** MD/PhD and other dual-degree options
- **Curriculum:** Core courses in neuroscience fundamentals, statistics, and research methods

### Training Opportunities
- **Laboratory Rotations:** Hands-on experience in diverse research environments
- **Core Facilities Training:** Access to state-of-the-art equipment and expert training
- **Technical Workshops:** Regular workshops on experimental techniques, data analysis, and programming
- **Professional Development:** Grant writing, scientific communication, and career planning

### Resources for Trainees
- **Mentorship Programs:** Structured mentoring from faculty and senior researchers
- **Funding Opportunities:** Information on fellowships, grants, and awards
- **Computing Resources:** Access to high-performance computing and data storage
- **Research Support:** Technical support, statistical consultation, and research design assistance

### Workshops & Courses
We offer regular training sessions on:
- Programming for neuroscience (Python, R, MATLAB)
- Data analysis and statistics
- Machine learning and AI applications
- Neuroimaging techniques
- Electrophysiology methods
- Molecular and cellular techniques
- Research ethics and responsible conduct of research
